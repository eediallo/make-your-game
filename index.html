How to Play

  • Start: Click the "Start" button to begin the game.
  • Pause: Press the P key to pause the game.
  • Continue: Press the C key to resume the game after pausing.
  • Reset: Press the R key to restart the game.
  • Move Left: Press the ← key to move the player Left.
  • Move Right: Press the → key to move the player Right.
  • Shoot: Press the Space key to shoot the enemies.
